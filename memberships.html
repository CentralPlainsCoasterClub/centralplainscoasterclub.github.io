Memberships

Why?

Central Plains Coaster Club works diligently to ensure that our members are given great benefits.  In prior events, all costs were taken out of pocket, which limited the quality of the events.  We have attempted to sell merchandise to help with expenses, but even then we lose money.  When we implement this new system, the majority of the costs will be covered by membership yields.  Whether you're choosing the Diamond membership or the Platinum membership, you will be able to experience awesome benefits and memorable events.  Even if you're a free member, you will still get to experience our active Facebook group, use it as a forum with fellow enthusiasts, and get to know the members of the club!

Benefits

If you choose not to partake in the memberships, you will be given the 'Standard' membership.  This includes access to the Facebook group and admission to some CPCC events.

If you wish to only attend a few events each year, the 'Diamond' membership may suit you best.  It is an annual membership that will expire on December 31, 2018.  This tier is comprised of park discounts at numerous parks located throughout the world, a random park map, and a membership card printed on 14-pt cardstock.

The 'Platinum' membership is meant for the most active park enthuasiasts, as it will grant its members the first slots to an event that has a limit on guests.  In addition, platinum level members will recieve five(5) random park maps, park discounts, and a plastic membership card.  Unlike the aforementioned tiers, the platinum card will never expire.
