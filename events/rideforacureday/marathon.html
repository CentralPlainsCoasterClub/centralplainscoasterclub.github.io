Marathon Details

One key event for RFACD is the coaster marathon.
This year's will take place on the classic Schwarzkopf Looping Star, Silver Bullet!

Similar to a Walk-A-Thon, those interested can collect a set dollar amount per ride cycle on Silver Bullet or a flat monetary donation from those interested.
The four(4) highest pledgers will be selected to marathon the coaster!
